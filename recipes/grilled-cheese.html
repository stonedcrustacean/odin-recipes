Ultimate Grilled Cheese

The humble grilled cheese, comfort food to many, pinacle of cuisine to others. Whatever your opinon may be, this is a simple, easy to cook dish that will see you satisfied and filled with motivation! Serve with a rich tomato soup or go the decadent route and slap a burger in there, conmplete with even more cheese

Ingredients:

  • 1 slice of sharp cheddar cheese
  • 1 slice of mozzarella cheese or a small handful of the dried, grated stuff
  • 2 thick slices of bread
  • 1 pinch of dried oregano
  • 1 pinch of salt and freshly ground black pepper
  • 2 tablespoons of salted butter (room temperature
  • 1 tablespoon of freshly chopped parsley
  • 1 teaspoon of freshly chopped chives

Steps

  1. Assmble the sandwich by placing cheddar and mozzarella cheese on the bread. Season with organo, salt, and pepper
  2. Heat butter in a pan over medium-low heat. Add sadnwish and cook until cheeses melt, this should take around 3 to 5 minutes, increase heat if necessary. Flip and cook until golden brown, 3 to 5 minutes more
  3. Cut in half and top with parsley and chives
