Tomato & Avocado Sandwich

It's time to use those avocados you bought last week and forgot about! Everyone's slapping avocados in everything these days, and while the hardest part about this recipe is catching your avocados in that 30 minute window of ripeness, the delightfully creamy, nutrient-rich berries (isn't botany strange?) will ensure you're feeling ready to tackle the day.

Ingredients

  • Half an avocado - peeled, pitted, and mashed
  • 1 tablespoon garlic salt
  • 1 slice ciabatta bread
  • 3 slices tomato
  • 1 pinch ground black pepper to taste

Step

  1. Mix mashed avocado and garlic salt together in a bowl; spread onto ciabatta bread. Layer tomatos over avocado and top with black pepper.
